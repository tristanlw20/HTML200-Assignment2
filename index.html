Responsive Web Design

What it means to me

Ever wonder how the websites you're viewing adjust to fit your screen size? Or how some websites know when to provide a mobile version of a page? All of this is accomplished by responsive web design. The importance of this concept is vast, allowing users to view and interact with content on the web regardless of their device. Anything from a Desktop, cell phone, or gaming console all have different ways of displaying content, but through responsive web design, developers are always able to provide the full experience of viewing their content. As an individual who travels a lot for work, this concept is very important to me. Being able to surf the internet, check emails, or chat with friends online while on the go is a must. Responsive web design doesn't just scale the items on a page to fit a screen either. No. It can change the page entierly. Things that were once in a square grid may now be aligned vertically. Buttons that were once large and colorful may now just be hyperlinks, squeezed together in a row. Its not just content placement either. Images can turn to text. Text can turn to sounds. The sky is the limit when it comes to responsive web design and developers are continuing to push the envelop and develop new ways to adapt and grow the internet across all devices.
